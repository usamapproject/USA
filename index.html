About

USA Map is a fan-made project for Euro Truck Simulator 2 focused on expanding and enhancing your driving experience. We provide high‑quality assets and community documentation to make setup simple and reliable. Because the project uses ATS assets, we require players to own American Truck Simulator and we verify this via Steam’s public API.

Key features
  • Curated, optimized assets for stable gameplay
  • Simple download flow with ownership verification
  • Clean documentation and support guidance
How it works
  1. Go to Download and enter your SteamID64.
  2. We securely check Steam’s API to confirm ATS ownership (no personal data is stored).
  3. If ownership is confirmed, ETS2 assets download unlocks immediately.
Privacy

Your SteamID64 is used only to verify ATS ownership via Steam’s public API and is not stored or shared.

FAQ
What is a SteamID64?

A 17‑digit unique identifier for your Steam account. You can find it at steamid.io.

Why do I need to verify ownership?

To ensure assets are used by players who own ATS, keeping distribution compliant with Steam’s terms.
