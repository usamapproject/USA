Requirements & Instructions

Requirements
  • Steam version of American Truck Simulator
  • Enough free disk space for all downloaded archives
  • Stable internet connection
Install steps
  1. Verify ATS ownership above and download all parts.
  2. Extract archives in order into your ETS2 mods folder.
  3. Enable the USA Map mod in ETS2 and restart.
Troubleshooting
  • Ownership failed? Double-check your 17‑digit SteamID64.
  • Downloads missing? Refresh after a minute and retry the check.
  • Game issues? Remove other conflicting mods and try again.
Support

For help, contact the team on our community channels. Please share your game version, a list of active mods, and screenshots of errors.
